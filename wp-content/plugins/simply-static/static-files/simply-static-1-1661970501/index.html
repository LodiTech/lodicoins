FAQs

What is Lodicoin?

LodiCoin is a Binance Smart Chain-based utility token that will drive the transactions on the LodiApp. Users can participate in the LodiCoin ICO sale to buy the tokens. Post the ICO sale, LodiCoins will be available on the LodiApp, where they can be bought in exchange for other cryptocurrencies.

How many is the token supply of Lodicoin?

Lodicoin minted 100,000,000,000 (100 billion) Token Supply

What is the utility of Lodicoin?

Lodicoin will be used for different transactions on the LodiApp. The use of Lodicoin for transactions will increase its utility, which will eventually contribute to its popularity and price in the future.
